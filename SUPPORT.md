# Support

**leenfetch** is a community-driven project. Here's how to get help or support the project.

## Community Support

- **Issues** — Report bugs or request features via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/drunkleen/leenfetch/issues)
- **Discussions** — Ask questions, share configs, or brainstorm ideas on [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/drunkleen/leenfetch/discussions)

If you find **leenfetch** valuable and want to support its development:

- **Buy Me a Coffee** — [buymeacoffee.com/drunkleen](https://buymeacoffee.com/drunkleen)

Your support helps cover hosting, CI costs, and development time. Thank you!
